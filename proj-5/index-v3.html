Football is a global team sport played between two teams of 11 players, whose objective is to score by getting the ball into the opponent's goal. It is a game of skill, strategy, and teamwork, requiring agility, stamina, and fast decision-making. Major tournaments like the FIFA World Cup are watched by billions, and the sport has a massive cultural impact. Whether played professionally or recreationally, it builds fitness, teamwork, and social skills
